Bellatrix Lestrange

Bellatrix is a graduate of the Paul Mitchell School and has been with Jeri’s since the beginning. She specializes in fades and beard trims that are so good you’d think she’s using magic. She is currently available Monday through Friday or by special appointment on Saturdays.

Bob Ross

Bob is a recent addition to Jeri’s family of barbers. Hailing from up north, he has over 20 years' experience with every style and cut out there. Bob is available on Mondays, Fridays, and Saturdays.

Walter White

Walter is a former chemistry teacher who changed careers five years ago and is now one of our best barbers. He can cook up any kind of style or cut you can imagine, but his specialties include head shaves and beard shaping. Walter is available Tuesday through Saturday.

Mr. T

Mr. T is a one of a kind barber who pities anyone with a bad haircut. Specializing in mohawks, fauxhawks, and head shaves, Mr. T is one of the best in the business and has been with Jeri’s since January. Mr. T is available Mondays, and Wednesday through Saturday.

Robert Gray

Robert is our newest addition to Jeri's family. He hails from Derry, Maine and just loves kids. He'll give your little one a cut just the way they like it. He also gives every child a free red ballon with haircut. Robert is available Monday through Friday.

Rey Smith

Rey has been with Jeri’s since we opened. She is truly a force to be reckoned with, with few equals out there in traditional cuts, full face shaves and shampoo massages. She is a graduate of Trident Technical College here in North Charleston and is available Monday, Wednesday, and Friday or by special appointment.
