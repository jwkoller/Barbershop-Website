Mvrck Products by Paul Mitchell

Jeri’s Old School Barbershop is proud to carry the Mvrck Men’s Care product line by Paul Mitchell. Mvrck Products were created by barbers with any eye toward functionality and authenticity.

Create versatile looks from classic, slick-backed hairstyles to the latest street styles with Original Pomade. The water-soluble formula won’t flake and washes out easily with shampoo.

Add volume, enhance texture and create sculpted hairstyles with Dry Paste. This matte finish, medium hold molding hair paste features a spin-off lid for no-fuss dispensing.

Create laid-back, undone looks with Grooming Cream. This quick-styling product adds light hold, soft definition and natural shine to air-dried hair or dry hair.

Designed to control and moisturize all beard styles and lengths, Skin + Beard Lotion shapes and tames coarse beard hair, from light stubble to full grown and everything in between.

Ideal for adding texture and volume to hair, Grooming Spray creates a variety of men’s hairstyles. This lightweight finishing hairspray features a trigger sprayer for no-fuss dispensing.

Double Hitter 2-In-1 men's shampoo and conditioner cleanses and conditions in one simple step. This multi-tasker creates a hearty lather for a deep clean that leaves your hair looking clean, healthy and thick.
