From personal service and quality haircuts and shaves, to haircare products and free beer. Get your look, your style, and your Man Card back at Jeri's Old School Barbershop.

Jeri’s Old School Barbershop was founded in 2017 with the goal of returning to the classic barbershop feel. Whether it’s a haircut, beard trim, or more, Jeri’s aims to make you love your look and style. No appointment is ever needed and there's always a free beer with a haircut.

In a relaxed atmosphere, Jeri’s serves all ages with personalized service and the pride that comes with loving what we do. We also carry a wide variety of the best hair and beard care products to help you keep your look going. Stop in and experience that old school barbershop feel at Jeri’s.
